SS Komagata Maru

Sori Vahegaru ji ki Fatheh

Please let us disembark

Soriman Jathedar Balhit Khalsa Diwan Vancouver di seva vikhe berate hai ke asi kul kharch Koma Gata Maru da jo tusi kharch karonge jad tak asi aap di naukri kar ke ada na kareye asi aap de galam har. Bar Bar aap nu takseed hai ke sanu peche na mode ya jaye. Har tarak Sanu maddad kar ke utar lavo. Asi heth likhe wale ikrar karde han ke asi $1000 wapse har tarak apri seva karan liye bharan sade pechle jai dad vicho le lavo, asi tayas han.

(Jathe das Balhit Khalsa Diwan Vancouver)

We would like to request Khalsa that till we repay you by working for you for the amount you send on Koma Gata Maru, we shall be your slaves. We request to you again and again that we should not be sent back. Please let us disembark by helping us by any means.

We the undersigned agree that we shall return $1000 even if we have to sell our ancestral land.

(Source: Prof. Malwinder Jit Singh)
Translated from Punjabi to English by Rimmy Ghuman
