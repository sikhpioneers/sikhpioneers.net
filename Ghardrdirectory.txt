The 
Ghadr Directory

Containing the names of persons who have taken part in the Ghadr Movement in North America.  Directory is arranged by the first name of the persons from A-Z.

Complied by 
The Director, Intelligence Bureau, Home Department, 
Government of India.  1917, 1934
