SUR SINGH GADDARI BABA MEMORIAL SOCIETY has been established to ensure that the history of our ancestors in North America is properly and suitably preserved and appreciated. The contribution of Gaddari Babas must be understood and appreciated by all those who are desirous of a complete understanding of their role in the movement to liberate India starting in the early 1900s. At this time, it has been decided to highlight the contributions of the Gaddari Babas of Sur Singh. A plot of land in the District Amritsar, Punjab, India, has been obtained to build a memorial centre to advance the understanding and the appreciation of the Gaddari Baba's history of struggle for us. The memorial centre shall house a museum and a library where our future generations shall be able to learn about the lives of patriot-martyrs such as Jagat Singh and Prem Singh, both of Sur Singh.
