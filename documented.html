The First 100 Documented Sikhs in Canada

Although the Sikhs started coming to Canada in 1897, their arrival was not officially documented until 1904. Shipping manifests for the year 1904 and 1905 contain the names of the first documented Sikhs who arrived in Canada as immigrants. They arrived by vessels such as The Empress of Japan, The Empress of China, The Empress of India, SS Athenian, R.M.S. Tartar, etc. The spellings of some names appear to be strange and inaccurate. However, they are reproduced here exactly from hand-written documents form as they appear in the shipping manifests located at the National Archives of Canada.
