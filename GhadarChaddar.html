Was it Ghadar or Chaddar Movement?

Call it a typographical error or an ostensible mistake the fact remains that for the past 20 years the Union government still continues to recognize the historical movement as, "Chaddar Movement". The brochure published by the Ministry of Home Affairs that was printed in 1980 after the introduction of the Swatantra Sainik Samman pension scheme, 1980, still continues to carry the name of an unheard movement "the Chaddar Movement" enlisted at serial number 12 while the name of Ghadar movement gets no mention in the entire list mentioning as many as 30 minor and major mutinies and movements.

HTC, Ludhiana -Wednesday May 2 2001- Hindustan Times New Dehli
